🏠 Founded in Mansa

How It All Started

It started with a problem every family in Mansa knows — finding good furniture meant travelling far, waiting weeks, or settling for whatever arrived in town.

The founders of Rapid Stores General Dealers Limited saw this gap firsthand. Families were spending too much time and money chasing down a decent mattress or a reliable TV. There had to be a better way — and the answer was right here in Mansa.

The idea was simple: stock the pieces Mansa homes actually need, price them honestly, and make ordering as easy as sending a WhatsApp message. No inflated margins. No confusing catalogues. Just real furniture, from real people, in your town.

We opened our doors at a single location and started small — a few mattresses, some storage pieces, and a phone number. Word spread the way it always does in a close-knit town: neighbour told neighbour. Orders came in. Trust was built one delivery at a time.

💡
"We didn't set out to build a big company. We set out to make sure our community had somewhere honest to buy furniture — and that's still what drives us today."

Today, Rapid Stores General Dealers Limited operates across three pickup points in Mansa — Hebron Plaza, Mansa Market, and Trade Centre — with a full digital catalogue and WhatsApp ordering that reaches customers across Luapula Province. But the spirit that started it all hasn't changed: we are your neighbours, and we take that seriously.

🏪 Our Story

Rapid Stores started with a simple idea: Mansa families deserve access to quality bedroom and living-room furniture without having to travel or wait for weeks. We set up shop in the heart of town to make that happen.

We are a locally-run store focused on the products that matter most in a Zambian home — a good mattress to sleep on, a reliable TV for family evenings, and practical storage that keeps the house organised. Every item we stock is chosen deliberately, not just to fill a catalogue.

From Hebron Plaza to Mansa Market and Trade Centre, we have made it easy to collect in person or arrange delivery across Mansa and nearby districts. We are a WhatsApp-first store — meaning you can browse, ask questions, and confirm an order all in one chat.
