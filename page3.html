❓ Frequently Asked Questions

No FAQs match your search.

How do I place an order?

Browse our catalogue on the home page, add items to your cart, then visit the Checkout page to enter your details. We'll confirm your order and payment via WhatsApp — it takes just a few minutes.

How do I pick up my order?

Once payment is confirmed, we'll WhatsApp you to let you know your items are ready. You can then collect from Hebron Plaza, Mansa Market, or Trade Centre — Monday to Saturday, 8 AM to 6 PM (Saturday until 2 PM). Pickup is always free.

What is your return and support policy?

We offer 7-day after-care from the date you collect. If something is damaged or not as described, WhatsApp us a photo of the issue and we'll arrange a replacement or resolve it — no lengthy forms.

Which pickup point is best for me?

Hebron Plaza is our main showroom and the most accessible. Mansa Market is centrally located. Trade Centre is ideal for larger items. WhatsApp us first and we'll tell you where your specific item is ready from.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept MTN MoMo and Airtel Money — pay via mobile money before or at collection. Cash on collection is also available by arrangement. Payment details are confirmed with you on WhatsApp before pickup.

How long does it take for my order to be ready?

Most items are available for same-day or next-day pickup. Once we confirm payment, we'll send you a WhatsApp message telling you when and where to collect. During busy periods it may take up to 24 hours.

Can I request a product not listed on your site?

Yes — send us a WhatsApp message describing what you're looking for, your budget, and your preferred size or style. We regularly source new items based on customer requests and will let you know if we can help.
