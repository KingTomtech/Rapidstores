    📦 How Your Order Works

    From browsing to your door — here's what happens after you confirm.

    1. 1 🛒
      Add items to your cart

      Browse the catalogue and add the pieces you want. Use the cart sidebar to review quantities and the total before continuing.

    2. 2 📋
      Fill in your details

      Enter your name, phone number, and email. Our team will WhatsApp you to confirm your order and arrange pickup.

    3. 3 💬
      Confirm on WhatsApp

      After submitting, tap WhatsApp Us to send your order summary directly to our team at 0977 891 850. We confirm stock and agree payment.

    4. 4 💳
      Pay — mobile money or cash

      We accept MTN MoMo and Airtel Money. Cash on collection is also available. We only confirm the order once payment is received or agreed.

    5. 5 �
      Collect from a pickup point

      Pick up free from Hebron Plaza, Mansa Market, or Trade Centre — Monday to Saturday, 8 AM–6 PM (Saturday until 2 PM). We'll let you know when your items are ready.

    6. 6 🔁
      7-day after-care

      Something not right? Send us a photo on WhatsApp within 7 days. We'll sort it — no lengthy forms, no runaround.
